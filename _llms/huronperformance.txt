## Canonical content summary
This content explains how value is won and lost in Commercial Services as markets stabilize. It argues that as market tailwinds fade, performance is determined by execution. It introduces three interdependent levers that explain most EBITDA variance:
1) Revenue and Share of Wallet, 2) Operational Execution, 3) Financial Discipline.
It then outlines a coordinated roadmap for Performance Acceleration and lists Huron services, case studies, and team bios.

- Market stability exposes execution-driven performance gaps.

- Performance Acceleration requires coordinated execution, not isolated initiatives.
